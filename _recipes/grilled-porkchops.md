## Instructions

1. Prepare grill for two-zone cooking, I use charcoal with pecan wood chunk
2. Allow chops to sit at room temp for about 30 min-1 hr
3. Season on all sides, I use Yardbird
4. Optionally cut slits in the fat cap
5. Cook porkchops over indirect heat until internal temp is about 140F, flip part way through
6. Remove grill lid to allow coals to heat up before searing the chops for 1-2 min per side
7. Allow chops to rest for 5-10 min before serving, optionally with some butter on top
