## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400F
2. Peel and cut carrots into 2" strips
3. Toss carrots in olive oil and seasonings
4. Place carrots on a baking sheet and spread them out
5. Roast in the oven for 30 min, stirring/tossing half-way through
