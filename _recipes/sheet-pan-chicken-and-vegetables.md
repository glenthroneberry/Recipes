## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a large sheet pan with foil or parchment.
2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper.
3. Pat chicken thighs dry and place on one side of the sheet pan. Rub about half the oil mixture over them.
4. Toss broccoli, carrots, and bell pepper with the remaining oil mixture, then spread on the other side of the pan.
5. Slice the lemon and tuck a few slices among the vegetables and chicken.
6. Roast for 30–35 minutes, until chicken reaches 165°F (74°C) internally and vegetables are tender and lightly browned.
7. Let rest 5 minutes before serving.

## Notes

Swap in whatever vegetables you have on hand — just cut denser ones (carrots, potatoes) smaller so they cook at the same rate as quicker vegetables like broccoli or peppers.
